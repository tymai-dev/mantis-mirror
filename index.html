Answers before you ride

Still curious? Here are some of the most common questions from early riders and commuters.

Why Mantis Mirror instead of other bicycle mirrors?

The Mantis Mirror System lets you view approaching traffic from any riding position without fiddling with your helmet or bars. Its dual optics keep the view stable so you can stay focused on the path ahead.

Does the mirror block forward vision?

The compact mirrors sit just outside your forward sightline, so you gain rear awareness without losing what is in front of you.

How do I install the system?

Attach the low-profile mount to your helmet using the included high-bond adhesive tape, then snap the mirror arm into place. Removal is just as effortless for storage.

Will it fit my helmet?

Yes. The adhesive base is designed to work with most modern helmet shells and shapes, creating a secure connection without tools.
